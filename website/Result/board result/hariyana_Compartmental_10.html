Haryana Board 10th / 12th Result 2025

  • Students who appeared for the Haryana Board 10th and 12th exams in 2025 can now view their results online.
  • To access the results, candidates must provide their correct credentials on the official result portal.
  • After the declaration of the results, it is the students' responsibility to check their Haryana Board 10th and 12th results for 2025. The results are available on the official website of the Board of School Education, Haryana (BSEH). Students should have their roll number and date of birth ready to access their marks. While the online result is a provisional marksheet, the original documents will be distributed by their respective schools at a later date.

Document Need to Check Result

  • To check your result for the Haryana Board 10th or 12th exams, you will need your roll number, which is printed on your admit card. The roll number is a unique identifier required to access your scorecard on the official website of the Board of School Education, Haryana (BSEH). Make sure to keep your admit card in a safe place so you can easily find the roll number when the results are announced.

Instructions For Checking Haryana Board 10th / 12th Result 2025

  • Visit the official website: Go to bseh.org.in.
  • Find the result link: On the homepage, click on the "Results" tab. Look for the specific link for your exam, such as "Secondary (Academic) Exam Result" for 10th grade or "Sr. Secondary (Academic) Exam Result" for 12th grade.
  • Enter your details: A new page will open where you'll need to enter your roll number and date of birth.
  • Submit and view: Click on the "Get Marks" or "Search Result" button. Your result will be displayed on the screen.
